Me

I am currently a post-doc in the Interaction Lab at Heriot-Watt University in Edinburgh. I recently finished my Ph.D. in computational linguistics at the Institute of Formal and Applied linguistics, Charles University in Prague.

My main research focus is machine learning and deep learning for natural language processing, especially human-computer dialogue and natural language generation, which was also the topic of my Ph.D. thesis.
